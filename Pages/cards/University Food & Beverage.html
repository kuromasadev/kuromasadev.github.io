 Aramark Student Food Venues

Gensler 2017
Sam Houston State University - Lowman Student Center (LSC)

Working with Aramark, developed and issued construction drawings from SD for five different venues. Two venues in a new construction (Oath Pizza, Steak n Shake) and three venues in a retrofitted space (Subway, Panda Express, Chick-fil-A).
